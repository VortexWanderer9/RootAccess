About me

Why do you want to know about me? Maybe the real question is: why do we seek to know anyone at all? I am not a genius, just a normal person—perhaps a side character in the movie of life, or maybe the main character, depending on perspective. Every day is a new scene, a new script, and I am just trying to play my part as best as I can. This page is just a creation—a reflection of my journey. Whether you find it interesting or not is beyond my control. In truth, what others think is fleeting; what matters is the authenticity of the self. We spend so much time wondering about ourselves like me, you are trying to make sense of your own story. I am not perfect, and I do not strive for perfection. Instead, I strive for growth, for understanding, for connection. Life is a series of questions, and sometimes the answers are less important than the act of asking. If you find something useful here, take it with you.
